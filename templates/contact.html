Frequently Asked Questions

We provide comprehensive food safety guidelines to all our partners. Donors are responsible for ensuring food is safe to eat, properly stored, and within its shelf life. NGOs are trained in safe food handling and transportation. We follow all local health department regulations.

FoodShare is completely free for both donors and NGOs. We believe in removing all barriers to food sharing. Our platform is supported by grants, donations, and corporate partnerships.

We accept cooked meals, packaged foods, fresh produce, baked goods, and non-perishable items. All food must be safe to eat and properly stored. We do not accept expired food or food that has been improperly stored.

Register on our platform as an NGO, provide your organization's details and documentation. Our team will review your application and verify your organization within 1-2 business days. Verified NGOs gain access to all available food listings.
